Paired Preference Assessment Tool for Videos

Instructions

Welcome to the Paired Preference Assessment Tool. This website is a tool designed for researchers and educators to assess the favorite rewards of autistic children without relying on verbal communication. Using a series of video trials, the children will be able to choose their preferred rewards without the need for speech.

To use this tool, first select the videos you would like to use by providing their YouTube URLs in the "Add New Videos" section. For each one, provide a custom title that determines how it will be referred to later. You will not be able to store more than 20 videos at once.

Next, choose the parameters of the test. You can adjust whether the videos are displayed horizontally or vertically and whether the test shows still images or video playback. You can also choose how long to wait (from 3 to 90 seconds) before skipping an option and moving on to the next one.

Once you're ready to begin, select which videos you want to include in the test using the set of checkboxes, then press the "Start Test" button.

After the test concludes, you'll see how often each video was selected and also a chart that displays these results visually. You can either copy the numbers from the results screen or download a PNG image file of the chart.
