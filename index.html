Paired Preference Assessment Tool for Videos

Instructions

Welcome to the Paired Preference Assessment Tool. This website is a tool designed for researchers and educators to assess the favorite rewards of autistic children. Using a series of video trials, subjects will be able to choose their preferred rewards without relying on verbal communication.

To use this tool, first imput the videos you would like to use by providing their YouTube URLs below. You can store a maximum of 20 videos. Next, choose the parameters of the test. You can adjust the orientation of the videos and whether the test shows video thumbnails or the video player. You can also choose how long to wait (from 3 to 90 seconds) before skipping a trial and moving on to the next one. Once you're ready to begin, select which videos you want to include in the test, then press "Start Test."

After the test concludes, you will see the percentage that each video was selected, and a chart that displays these results. You can either copy the data or download a PNG image file of the chart.
